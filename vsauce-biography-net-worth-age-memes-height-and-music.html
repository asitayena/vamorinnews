Michael David Stevens is an American educator, public speaker, comedian, entertainer, editor, and internet celebrity who is popular for creating and hosting the popular education YouTube channel Vsauce.

Michael Stevens was born on January 23, 1986, in Kansas City, Missouri, United States.

He graduated from the University of Chicago with a bachelor’s degree in psychology and English literature.

Stevens is currently married to Marnie and the couple now resides in Los Angeles.
