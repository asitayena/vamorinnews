FAQs about Kaya Yanar

When and where was Kaya Yanar born?

He was born on May 20, 1973, in Frankfurt am Main, Germany.

What is he known for?

He is a renowned actor and writer, best known for his work on the comedy show Was guckst du?!.

What are some of his most popular shows or performances?

He gained popularity through TV shows such as Was guckst Du? (2001 – 2005), Die Kaya Show (2012), Typisch Deutsch?! (2013), and Geht’s noch?! Kayas Woche (2014).

What is his ethnic background?

His parents are of Turkish-Arab descent and lived in Antakya, Southern Turkey, before emigrating to Germany.

Is he involved in any social causes?

Yes, he is a vegan and an active supporter of animal rights, particularly associated with the organization PETA.

Has he written any books?

Yes, he has authored a book titled Made in Germany, which is available in German edition.

What awards has he received?

He has won several awards, including the German Television Award for Best Comedy Program and the German Comedy Award for Best Comedy/Variety Show for Was guckst du?! among others.

Does he have any music-related works?

While primarily known for his comedy, Kaya also has music tracks like Planet Deutschland – Teil 45 and Planet Deutschland – Teil 04.

What languages can Kaya Yanar speak?

Despite his Turkish nationality, he has stated that he cannot speak Turkish well. He learned pidgin-English for one of his sketches by practicing with Indian friends from university.

What is his educational background?

He attended a humanistic grammar school in Frankfurt and later studied phonetics, American studies, and philosophy at university but did not graduate.
