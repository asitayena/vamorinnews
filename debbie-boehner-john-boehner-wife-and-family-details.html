Debbie Boehner: John Boehner Wife And Family Details

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 23, 2024

After the accusation made against Donald Trump about the insurrection, many are interested in John Boehner and his wife, Debbie Boehner.

Debbie Boehner is an American real estate agent popularly known for being the wife of John Andrew Boehmer, an American speaker and 53rd speaker of “The House of Representatives.” 

After the release of her husband’s book “On the House: A Washington Memoir,” he had heavily criticized his fellow politicians: addressing the insurrection on January 6th.

This was a huge surprise to the people as the speaker been a long-time friend of Donald Trump.

Debbie Boehner: John Boehner Wife

Debbie Boehner is the wife of John Andrew Boehner. They got married in Hamilton County, Ohio, in the year 1973. 

The pair met while they both were in college. Having graduated from “Mount Healthy High School,” she enrolled at the “University of Cinncinati,” which is where they first met. 

John Boehner never stopped her from doing what she wanted to. While he was to be named the most powerful politician in American, Debbie was working as a cashier at a local gardening store.

The independent woman is now working as a real estate agent. In turn, she trusts her husband. 

Back in 2010, when John was accused of an affair with Lisbeth Lyons, Debbie had faith in her husband, and thankfully her faith was true as the affair was a hoax.
