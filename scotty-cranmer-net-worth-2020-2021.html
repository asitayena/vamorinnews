Early Life

Scotty Cranmer was born on 11 January 1987 in Jackson Township, New Jersey, to parents Dona and Scot. He has a younger brother named Matt, who is seen as a regular guest on Scotty’s YouTube channel. He passed his high school from Jackson Memorial High school, and just after that, at the age of 18, he became a professional BMX rider.

His daring and never-say-die attitude earned him the nickname of “The Bulldozer” from his friends and colleagues. During his journey as a professional rider, Scotty has been in a number of serious accidents for which he has undergone various surgeries.

One of his surgeries, following a horrific accident while shooting for a Monster Energy drink commercial, was shared on his social media, where millions of fans supported and donated for him through various campaigns. Scotty is married to Lisa, his partner for life, and lives along with his parents and a pet dog.

Career

Due to the unprecedented success Scotty has witnessed as a rider, many high profile brands, like ‘Hyper Bikes,’ ‘Vans Shoes,’ ‘Fox Clothing,’ ‘Monster Energy Drink,’ ‘Protech cement’ and ‘Snafu’ to name a few, sponsor him. He has boosted their products by using them in most of his competitions and also on his YouTube channel. Apart from this, he also endorses these brands.

Scotty Cranmer is also the proud owner of a bike shop named ‘SC Action Sports Bicycle’ in New Jersey. Moreover, he has also appeared as himself in many movies, like ‘Illustrated,’ ‘Hella crazy,’ and ‘Next X,’ where he performed daring bike stunts.
