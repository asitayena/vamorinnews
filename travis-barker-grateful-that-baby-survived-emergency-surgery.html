Travis Barker so grateful that baby survived life-threatening emergency surgery

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Travis Barker expressed his gratitude after he and wife Kourtney Kardashian’s unborn baby boy survived an emergency surgery.

“I flew home for a life-threatening emergency surgery for our baby that I’m so grateful went well,” the Blink-182 drummer wrote via Twitter Wednesday.

“I want to say thank you for all the support,” Barker continued, adding that Blink-182’s European tour — which was delayed so that Barker could fly home to be with Kardashian — would resume on Friday.

“God is great,” Barker added.

Barker’s tweet comes after the Poosh owner posted her own update on the “urgent fetal surgery” she had to undergo last week.

“I will be forever grateful to my incredible doctors for saving our baby’s life,” Kardashian, 44, posted on Instagram alongside a black-and-white photo of Barker holding her hand.

.

“I am eternally grateful to my husband who rushed to my side from tour to be with me in the hospital and take care of me afterwards, my rock. And to my mom, thank you for holding my hand through this.”

The mom of three shared that she had a “whole new understanding and respect” for women who have dealt with pregnancy complications, recalling that she was struck with “fear” as she was rushed to the hospital to undergo the life-saving operation.

“Walking out of the hospital with my baby boy in my tummy and safe was the truest blessing. 🙏🏼🤍.”

In photos obtained by Page Six, Barker, 47, and Kardashian were spotted leaving the hospital together on Saturday, a day after Blink-182 tweeted that Barker had to return home ahead of their European tour due to an “urgent family matter.”

An insider told People on Monday that Kardashian’s hospital stay was “brief” and she was already back home with her children and family.

“She is happy to have Travis back home, too,” the source added.

Barker and Kardashian revealed that they were expecting their first child together during the “All the Small Things” drummer’s Los Angeles show in June.

Kardashian shares three children — sons Mason, 13, and Reign, 8, and daughter Penelope, 11 — with ex Scott Disick.

Barker shares son Landon, 19, and daughter Alabama, 17, with ex-wife Shanna Moakler. He is also stepfather to Moakler’s daughter Atiana, 24, who the model shares with ex Oscar de la Hoya.
