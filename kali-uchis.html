Overall Meaning

The lyrics of Kali Uchis's song "Tomorrow" convey a sense of hope, freedom and living in the present moment. The opening lines serve as a manifestation of the singer's newfound belief in miracles and breaking free from the curse that has held her back. She acknowledges that no one can give her what she wants, but this does not deter her from seeking a better life.


The subsequent lines offer an escape from the present situation as the singer urges her lover to leave this hopeless town with her, assuring that nobody can stop them. The phrase "Let's think about it tomorrow" is repeated, emphasizing the need to live in the present and not dwell on the future or past. The following verse tells the story of a young girl who had to turn to prostitution to support her family. It further emphasizes the theme of seeking a better life and not letting one's circumstances hold them down.


The chorus reiterates the need to leave behind their present situation and seek a better life, with the singer calling for them to burn together like a comet in the sky. The song's lyrics are a call for change and a better future, with the emphasis on living in the present and taking action to create the future they desire.
