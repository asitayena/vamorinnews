Victoria's Secret Photoshop Fail Removes a Model's Butt Cheek: See It

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, September 1, 2024

A little less bootylicious? Victoria's Secret shared a photo of a model working her backside via Facebook on Friday, Sept. 25—par for the course for the lingerie brand. The problem? One of her butt cheeks seems to have been Photoshopped out!

The brunette model, who's facing away from the camera, can be seen wearing a pair of teal, semi-sheer lace underwear, cut in a "cheeky" style to show off her assets. But there's nothing to see, as the curve of her left butt cheek has been entirely removed from the photo.

Within minutes, Facebook users started commenting on the pic—and they were not happy. One commenter said, "Can we stop making women's bodies objects to be altered, color changed, resized, chopped up?" while another wrote, "I can't believe you would even photoshop your models. They work so hard for the perfect bodies they have, then you mess it up with photoshop. Dumb."

Many more then stated that they'd be taking their business elsewhere, notably to Aerie, the lingerie brand that recently launched a Photoshop-free campaign starring Emma Roberts.
