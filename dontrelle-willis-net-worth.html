What is Dontrelle Willis’s Net Worth and Salary?

Early Life and Education

Dontrelle Willis was born on January 12, 1982, in Oakland, California, to parents Earl and Angelic Willis. He grew up in Alameda, California, where he attended Encinal High School. There, he excelled in both baseball and basketball.

After high school, Willis received a scholarship to play baseball at the University of Arizona. However, he only played one year and left college to pursue a career in professional baseball.

Career

Willis began his professional baseball career in 2000 when he was drafted by the Chicago Cubs in the eighth round of the Major League Baseball (MLB) draft. He made his MLB debut in 2003 with the Florida Marlins, where he quickly became a fan favorite.

Willis had his best season in 2005 when he won 22 games and was named an All-Star. He finished second in the Cy Young Award voting that year. Throughout his career, Willis also played for the Detroit Tigers, Arizona Diamondbacks, and Cincinnati Reds.

After his playing career, Willis opened a restaurant called The D Shop in Coral Springs, Florida. He also invested in real estate and became a baseball analyst for Fox Sports Florida.

Personal Life

Real Estate

Legacy
