Bella Thorne, Gigi Hadid Stun on Much Music Video Awards Red Carpet

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Bella Thorne stole the red carpet at the 2015 Much Music Video Awards on Sunday, June 21, as she stunned crowds with her bold, red lace dress complete with a top-to-bottom zipper.

The Scream actress, who walked the carpet with new love Gregg Sulkin, 23, (who recently began dating his longtime friend, as Us Weekly reported) smoldered as she cuddled up with her new man, posing for pictures as they entered the Canadian awards ceremony.

Gigi Hadid also caused a stir as she stepped out onto the carpet dressed in a matching pale pink skirt and top combo, which showed off her toned stomach and skinny legs. She accompanied her cropped outfit with a white leather jacket, with her name, Hadid, written out on the back.

Other celebs standing out from Toronto’s red carpet crowd included Debby Ryan, who arrived with her new band, The Never Ending. Looking gorgeous in a black culotte pantsuit, a source tells Us she was overwhelmed by the big names around her. “Gigi Hadid was standing next to her but was too shy to say hello, even though they have lots of friends in common,” one red-carpet observer revealed.
