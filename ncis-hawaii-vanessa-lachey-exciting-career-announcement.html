NCIS: Hawai'i star Vanessa Lachey makes exciting career announcement

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, September 7, 2024

The Jane Tennant actress took to Instagram

    While Vanessa Lachey is a familiar face to many thanks to her starring role on NCIS: Hawai'i, she is also known for presenting Netflix's Love Is Blind with her husband, Nick Lachey - and now the actress has made a very exciting announcement regarding the reality show. 

    Taking to her Instagram account, the 42-year-old star revealed that is returning with a brand new series this month!

    Sharing a snap of her and Nick, the mom-of-three wrote in the caption: "NEW #LoveIsBlind March 24!!! New Season! New City! New Couples! New Love! Same ole Lacheys!!! Who’s ready for Season 4?!

    "Swipe to meet the cast."
