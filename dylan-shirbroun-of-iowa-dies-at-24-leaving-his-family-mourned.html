Dylan Shirbroun of Iowa dies at 24, leaving his family mourned, GoFundMe raises $15k

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Dylan Shirbroun, Iowa – The Shirbroun family is grappling with the devastating and unexpected loss of their cherished son, Dylan Shirbroun. At the tender age of 24, Dylan passed away, leaving his family and the community of Coon Rapids, Iowa, in deep sorrow.

Dylan was a young man whose kindness and gentle spirit were simply unparalleled. His smile had the power to light up any room, and his absence will leave an irreplaceable void in the hearts of all who had the privilege of knowing and loving him.

At the time of this publication, no official cause of death has been reported. The Shirbroun family, while confirming the heartbreaking news, is coming to terms with their loss. Dylan leaves behind a loving family: his mother, Trina; his father, Josh; his sisters, Shayla and Ciara; and his brother, Mason. He is also survived by his grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, and a multitude of friends whose lives were touched by his presence.

Dylan embodied the essence of a remarkable individual. His innate qualities and character traits left an indelible mark on the lives he touched. Perhaps one of his most remarkable attributes was his unwavering sense of calm. In any situation, he exuded a soothing and reassuring presence that provided solace to those around him. It was as though he possessed a natural talent for alleviating stress and anxiety with a simple smile or a kind word.

Kenadi Songer launched a GoFundMe campaign with the target of $15,000 which states;

“It is with heavy hearts that we announce the tragic loss of Dylan Shirbroun at the young age of 24. Dylan was a a kind, gentle soul and his smile lit up the room. His absence will leave big holes in the hearts of all those who knew him and loved him. Dylan leaves behind his mom; Trina, dad; Josh, sister’s; Shayla and Ciara, and brother; Mason. He also leaves behind his grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, and friends. His family is now trying to navigate life without him. Any help with final expenses is greatly appreciated”.

Dylan wasn’t just a friend; he was an inspiration. His boundless enthusiasm for life and his unwavering optimism served as a wellspring of motivation for those fortunate enough to know him. He exemplified how kindness and authenticity could make a profound impact on people and communities. His life was a testament to the power of spreading positivity and embracing one’s uniqueness.

To Dylan’s friends, he was a perpetual source of laughter and joy. His infectious laugh had the magical ability to brighten even the darkest of days. His readiness for a good laugh was a testament to his light-hearted spirit and his remarkable ability to find humor in life’s everyday moments.

As Coon Rapids and the surrounding communities grieve the loss of Dylan Shirbroun, they hold onto the precious memories and the enduring legacy of a young man who left an indelible mark of kindness, authenticity, and optimism on the world.
