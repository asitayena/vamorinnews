Who is Patricia Clarkson?Her Age, Sisters, Husband, Net Worth

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, September 5, 2024

With over 3 decades of brilliant film, television, and stage acting to her name, Patricia Clarkson is indeed a bonafide Hollywood icon. This very talented actress has received a lot of critical acclaim during her long career and they include an Oscar nomination, a Golden Globe Award, 2 Primetime Emmy Awards, a Tony Award nomination and 5 Screen Actors Guild Award nominations.

After beginning her acting career in the 1980s, Clarkson starred in several feature films throughout the 90s and has recorded countless films to date. She also had starring roles in the 1990s television series Davis Rules and Murder One. She received critical acclaim in the early 2000s when she played the recurring role of Sarah O’Connor on the HBO series Six Feet Under. She has become known lately for her role in the hit Netflix series House of Cards and the HBO thriller series Sharp Objects

Who is Patricia Clarkson? Age

Patricia Davies Clarkson was born in New Orleans, Louisiana on December 29, 1959. She was born and raised in Algiers, a New Orleans community located on the west bank of the Mississippi. There she attended O. Perry Walker High School and upon graduation she enrolled to study speech therapy at Louisiana State University. She transferred to Fordham University after some time and there she pursued and completed an undergraduate degree in acting. She also earned a Master of Fine Arts from the Yale School of Drama.

Clarkson began her acting career on stage to mark her debut in a 1986 Broadway production of The House of Blue Leaves. A year later, she got her first film role playing in the gangster film Les Incorruptibles (1987). She landed her first major role opposite Clint Eastwood in the action film The Dead Pool (1988). The early and mid-90s were turbulent for the actress as she was unable to secure major acting jobs. The talented actress finally overcame this difficult period in her career when she starred in the independent film High Quality Art (1998) which earned her many rave reviews.

After her stellar performance in High Quality Art, Patricia Clarkson’s acting career ramped up in the late 90s and early 2000s. She landed a major role in the HBO series Six Feet Under ( 2002-06) with recurring character SarahO’Connor and for her work on the hit series, the talented actress won 2 Primetime Emmy Awards. Around the same time, Clarkson was nominated for a Screen Actors Guild Award for her performance in the comedy film The Station Agent (2003) followed by an Oscar and Golden Globe nomination for her role. in another comedy Pieces of April (2003).

Upon her return to the stage in 2014, Clarkson starred in the Broadway production of The Elephant Man and her performance earned her a Tony Award nomination for Best Featured Actress. Her next notable role was in the British comedy film The Party (2017) for which she received the British Independent Film Award (BIFA) for Best Supporting Actress. Continuing her winning ways, the talented actress starred alongside Amy Adams in the HBO thriller mini-series Sharp Objects (2018) and her performance won the Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Series. , a mini-series or a television movie.

Parents and sisters
