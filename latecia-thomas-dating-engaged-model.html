Is Latecia Thomas Dating Or Engaged? Here Is The Quick Update About The Model!

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Latecia Thomas is an Aussie model who was born with a mixed ethnicity of Indian and Australian origin. There are few assumptions and rumors about the love life of the model. She recently appeared on the Super Bowl Weekend with Lil Wayne.

Latecia Thomas Body-Positive activist and fashion YouTuber

The model from Australia is also a body-positive activist and a fashion YouTuber. Latecia who grew up in Melbourne, Australia currently resides in Los Angeles, California.

The model inspires her 1.2 million followers to love their bodies. She also shows there is no lack of choices in the clothes option for the plus-size women out there.

She shares pictures modeling swimwear and figure-hugging fashion on Instagram. The model shows that curvaceous women don’t have to limit themselves to only loose and camouflaging outfits.

Back in 2017, she shared old and new pictures of herself on Instagram which shows her physical transformation. Thomas also appeared in the bikini competition in 2019.

However, she also struggled with insecurities and the struggle to maintain her body at a time. It got to the point where she stopped looking at the mirrors. Meanwhile, she overcame her insecurities and is not happy with her body. She also wrote her side of the story saying:

“I prefer me at any weight as long as I’m happy. It’s okay to love yourself no matter what your size is,”

Alongside inspiring her Instagram followers, she is also a YouTuber. Social media has helped her to grow in popularity in various aspects. Back in 2015, the model stepped into the social media career learning to click her makeup artistry. She calls herself a self-taught makeup artist.

The model shares her style with different brands offering plus-size clothing. She also motivates people to embrace their skin by showing her freckles on the camera.

Her relationship rumors

Back in 2019, there were rumors Thomas and Lil Wayne is dating each other.

However, neither of them has confirmed their relationship in the media. Afterward, in late 2019’s the model started rocking a huge ring on her finger which led to the rumor of their engagement.

After the rumors of dating in 2020, the alleged couple hit the streets of Miami on the SuperBowl weekend. The rapper celebrated his latest studio album at a private yacht party with the model. In the yacht, the rapper did a rap with the lyrics which says:

“Stop Playin With Me. Ooh, I got a plus-sized model/But she my lil’ mama/Imakeher bust itopen for me like a piñata/Andas the world turned, she was my spin doctor.”

Though the duo has not officially announced their relationship, it seems like the rapper is dropping out some hints. Meanwhile, the model is still silent about everything.
