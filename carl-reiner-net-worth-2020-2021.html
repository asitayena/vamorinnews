Early Life & Biography

Reiner was born to Irving, a watchmaker, and Bessie Reiner. His parents were Jewish immigrants. His father was from Austria, and his mother was from Romania. His older brother Charlie served in World War II, and his ashes are buried at Arlington National Cemetery. Charlie read in the New York Daily News about a free dramatic workshop being put on by the Works Progress Administration when he was just 16. He then told Carl about it. His uncle Harry Mathias was the first comedian in his family. He had previously been working as a machinist, repairing sewing machines.
