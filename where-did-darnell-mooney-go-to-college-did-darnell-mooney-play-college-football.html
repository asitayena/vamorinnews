Where did Darnell Mooney go to college? Did Darnell Mooney play college football?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, September 2, 2024

Darnell Mooney is a famous American football wide receiver, who is currently playing for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League. Darnell was born on October 29, 1997, in Gadsden, Alabama. He stands at a height of 5 feet and 11 inches and has a proportional body mass of 79 kg.

Let us know more about him, taking into consideration the college he attended, and find out if he played collegiate football.

Where did Darnell Mooney go to college?

Darnell attended Tulane University, where he was part of the Tulane Green Wave Football team.

Before going to Tulane University, Darnell Mooney had his High School education at Gadsden City High School in Gadsden, Alabama. While in High school, he played football and basketball for the Gadsden City Titans.

Did Darnell Mooney play college football?

Yes!, Darnell played college football at Tulane. While at Tulane university, he led the Green Wave in receiving in both his junior and senior seasons. He caught 48 passes each year, totaling 993 yards in 2018 and 713 yards in 2019.

He scored 19 touchdowns in his college career. Darnell ran a 40-yard dash time at the NFL draft combine of 4.38 seconds. Darnell was selected by the Chicago Bears with the 173rd pick in the fifth round of the 2020 NFL Draft. He signed a four-year rookie contract with the team on July 21.
