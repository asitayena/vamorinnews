FAQs about Cameron Diaz

Who is Cameron Diaz?

What is Diaz’s background?

She was born on August 30, 1972, in San Diego, California. She is of Cuban, Italian, and German descent. She grew up in Long Beach, California, and attended Long Beach Polytechnic High School.

When did Diaz start her acting career?

She began her acting career in 1994 with a role in The Mask after working as a model in her earlier years.

Is Cameron Diaz married?

Yes, she is married to Benji Madden, a musician from the band Good Charlotte. They got married in 2015.

Does Cameron Diaz have children?

Yes, she and Benji Madden have two children, a daughter named Raddix Madden, born in 2019, and a son named Cardinal Madden, born in 2024.

What books has Diaz written?

She has authored books on health and wellness, including The Body Book, which focuses on empowering women to lead healthier lives through better understanding of their bodies.

Has Diaz retired from acting?

She announced her retirement from acting in 2018 but has since decided to return to acting with the upcoming film Back in Action.

What are Diaz’s hobbies?

She is known to have a passion for cooking and wellness. She also enjoys surfing and golf.

What philanthropic work does Diaz engage in?

She is involved in various environmental and educational charities. She has worked with organizations like the Environmental Media Association and the Innocence Project.
