Lady Gaga Announces Her Own Las Vegas Residency

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Lady Gaga will join the list of superstars with regularly scheduled shows in Las Vegas.

Gaga announced Tuesday (Dec. 19) that she will join the list of superstars with regularly scheduled shows in Las Vegas, where she will begin a two-year special engagement at the 5,300-seat Park Theater beginning in December 2018.

“It has been a life-long dream of mine to play Las Vegas. I’ll tell you exactly what I told my MGM and Park Theater partners – you can count on this performer always for one thing…I’ll leave my heart on the stage every single night.” – Lady Gaga said.

Rumors surrounding major gig have been circulating for months. However, Gaga’s confirmation only came one day after she wrapped up her latest world tour in Los Angeles.

“The rumors are true!”

Performance dates and ticket information for Lady Gaga’s residency will be available in the coming months.
