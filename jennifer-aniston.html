Jennifer Aniston News - Us Weekly

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Name:Jennifer Aniston

Hometown:Sherman Oaks, California

Relationship:Single

Birthday:February 11, 1969

About

The daughter of actors John Aniston and Nancy Dow, Jennifer Aniston is best known for portraying Rachel Green on Friends from 1994 to 2004 and is one of the highest-paid actresses in Hollywood. Since the popular NBC sitcom ended, she has starred in a slew of hit films, including The Break-Up (2006), Marley & Me (2008), Just Go With It (2011), Horrible Bosses (2011), We’re the Millers (2013), Cake (2014) and Dumplin’ (2018). Aniston returned to television for the first time since Friends when The Morning Show premiered on Apple TV+ in 2019. Her Morning Show costar and real-life friend Reese Witherspoon previously played Rachel’s sister on Friends.

Aniston’s personal life has fascinated fans since her high-profile, five-year marriage to Brad Pitt ended in 2005. Shortly after their split, Pitt started dating his costar Angelina Jolie, causing intense debate among fans of the actresses.

Aniston moved on with Vince Vaughn from 2005 to 2006 and dated John Mayer from 2008 to 2009 before sparking a romance with Justin Theroux in 2011. The two married in 2015 but separated two years later, with their divorce being made final in 2018.
